Our Mission

We are a group of people who love music, musicians, and especially musical instruments. Our sole aim is to provide a service for those who love music in their homes in the form of a piano, and wish to delight them when the time comes for them to relocate their household piano to another place. We are here for you and your piano relocation needs!
